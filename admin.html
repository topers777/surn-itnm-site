Admin: REDCap Data & Provider Dashboards

Study team use only. Upload a REDCap CSV export to generate personalized dashboard links for each provider.

🗺 How the Registry Connects

The three parts of this site are intentionally separate — registration, dashboards, and the patient enrollment page are independent workflows:

📝 Provider Registration

MDs fill out the form on the Provider Portal. Submissions are sent directly to the study team email via Formspree. Nothing is stored in the browser.

📊 Provider Dashboards

Separate from registration. Upload a REDCap CSV here → generate a unique link per provider → email each provider their link. No login needed.

📱 Patient Enrollment

The QR code on the Provider Portal links patients to enroll.html. Patients click through to the REDCap consent survey. No connection to registration.

🔄 REDCap Data Updates

REDCap doesn't connect directly to this site. Export a fresh CSV weekly (or as needed) and re-upload here to generate updated dashboard links.

✉ Registration Submissions — Formspree Setup
ℹ
Provider registrations are emailed to the study team via Formspree (free). You can also view and export all submissions at formspree.io.
  1. Go to formspree.io → sign up free → New Form → set the notification email to SUFUResearch@stanford.edu
  2. Copy your Form ID (looks like xpzgkwqv) from the Formspree dashboard
  3. Open assets/js/app.js and replace YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID on the Formspree line with your form ID
  4. Done — all future registrations will be emailed immediately. Past submissions are visible at formspree.io
🤖 reCAPTCHA Setup
  1. Go to google.com/recaptcha → reCAPTCHA v2 → “I’m not a robot” checkbox → add your site's domain
  2. Copy the Site Key (public) and open index.html
  3. Replace YOUR_RECAPTCHA_SITE_KEY in the data-sitekey attribute with your site key
  4. Copy the Secret Key and add it to your Formspree settings under “reCAPTCHA” to enable server-side validation

🌐 Getting an Easy URL (Patient Enrollment Link)

Currently the site only works as a local file. To get a shareable link like surn-itnm.netlify.app/enroll for the patient QR code, deploy to Netlify (free):

  1. Go to netlify.com → sign up free → Add new site → Deploy manually
  2. Drag the entire surn-itnm-site folder onto the Netlify drop zone
  3. Netlify gives you a URL like random-name-12345.netlify.app — click Site settings → Change site name to set it to something like surn-itnm
  4. Update the reCAPTCHA allowed domains to include your new Netlify domain
  5. The patient enrollment URL becomes https://surn-itnm.netlify.app/enroll.html — the QR code on the Provider Portal auto-updates to this URL once deployed
