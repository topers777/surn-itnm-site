👥 Am I Eligible?

  • ✓Age 18 or older
  • ✓Receiving an iTNM implant (Revi™, eCoin™, Altaviva™, or similar) for bladder leakage
  • ✓Able to read English
  • ✓Have a phone or email for reminders
  • ✓Willing to complete surveys over one year

🧬 About the Study

A national registry tracking real-world outcomes for bladder implant devices, sponsored by the SUFU Research Network.

  • •IRB-approved, minimal risk
  • •5 short surveys, 10–15 minutes each
  • •Reminders by text and/or email
  • •Withdraw at any time, no penalty

What kinds of questions will I be asked?

Bladder Symptoms

Urgency frequency, urination frequency, and leakage episodes per day.

Quality of Life

How bladder symptoms affect daily activities, sleep, and social life.

Pad & Product Use

Daily pad use and how it changes after your implant.

Device Experience

Device usage, satisfaction, and any side effects or additional procedures.
